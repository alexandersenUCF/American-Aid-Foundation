Mobile Theater Unit

Bringing the Theater Experience to Those in Need

The American Aid Foundation will be bringing the full movie theater experience, complete with High Definition Front Projection and Surround Sound, to those children, seniors and Veterans who are unable to go to the theater, whether it be health, developmentally delayed, economic or other of life's hardship reasons. Help us bring hours of enjoyable entertainment experiences into those in need with a generous donation to the American Aid Foundation through the donation button below or send a check to our mailing address.

Major donors to this program will be displayed permanently on the Mobile Disaster Response Unit (truck) they donated to as well inclusion on the website and in literature we distribute.

The Mobile Theater Unit is starting as a pilot program in the Chicago area with two units and then will expand to other major metropolitan areas nationwide as we move forward.

Major donors to this program will be displayed permanently on the Mobile Theater Unit (van) they donated to as well inclusion on the website and in literature we distribute.

Leave a lasting legacy to your generosity by helping us bring this wonderful program to so many who need to enjoy a night of movie going to escape from the troubles they are dealing with.
