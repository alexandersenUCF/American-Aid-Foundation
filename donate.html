Donate

The American Aid Foundation offers donors many ways to make donations to support our various humanitarian programs. Below are ways donors can either donate monetarily or donate in ways like giving a percentage of money raised from eBay auctions or even making donations simply by shopping online at Amazon.com. The American Aid Foundation is a 501 (c)(3) Tax Exempt organization that allows all donors to take tax deductions for anything donated to the Foundation.


Start an eBay auction and donate all or a percentage of the auction proceeds to the American Aid Foundation.

Donate to the American Aid foundation using PayPal or any major credit card. 100% of the proceeds will go towards one of the many projects the American Aid Foundation facilitates.

With AmazonSmile you simply shop as you always do at Amazon.com (same products / same price) but you setup the American Aid Foundation as your charity of choice and shop on www.smile.amazon.com and everything you buy will lead to a 0.5% donation.

You may also make monetary donations by simply sending a check or money order (Payable to the American Aid Foundation) to 296 Lincoln Terrace, Buffalo Grove, IL 60089. If you wish to earmark the funds to a specific program indicate so on the memo part of your check or money order.
